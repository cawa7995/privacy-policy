Privacy Policy

Spark Property Management LLC respects your privacy.

Information Collection

We collect mobile phone numbers from contracted service providers and internal staff for work-related communication. Phone numbers are collected during physical contractor onboarding through express written consent.

Use of Information

Phone numbers are used solely to send operational and transactional SMS messages related to property management, including cleaning schedules and urgent property issues. We do not send marketing or promotional messages.

No Sharing Policy

Mobile information will not be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

Opt-Out

Recipients may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP.

For questions about this policy, contact Spark Property Management LLC.
