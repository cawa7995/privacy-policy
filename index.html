Privacy Policy

Spark Property Management LLC respects your privacy.

We collect mobile phone numbers from contracted service providers and internal staff for work related communication. Phone numbers are collected during contractor onboarding or through direct consent.

Phone numbers are used solely to send operational and transactional SMS messages related to property management. These messages may include cleaning schedules, schedule updates, cancellations, access instructions, and urgent property issues. We do not send marketing or promotional messages.

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Information is used only for internal operational communication.

Recipients may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP.

For questions about this policy, contact Spark Property Management LLC.
